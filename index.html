Minesweeper

Minesweeper is a puzzle video game.[1] In the game, mines (that resemble naval mines in the classic theme) are scattered throughout a board, which is divided into cells.

How to play : A player selects a cell to open it.

  • If a player opens a mined cell, the game ends.
  • Otherwise, the opened cell displays a number, indicating the number of mines diagonally
  • Or a blank tile (or "0"), and all adjacent non-mined cells will automatically be opened.
